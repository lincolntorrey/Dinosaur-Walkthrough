How to Survive Dinosaurs

  • Learn what kinds of dinosaurs you may encounter by reading about them on Wikipedia.
  • Stay hidden in dense foliage and keep your movement slow and controlled. Camouflage tips can be found in the Camouflage article.
  • Understand predator behavior. Many carnivorous dinosaurs may rely on movement to detect prey. Read about Tyrannosaurus for examples.
  • Use high ground whenever possible. Elevated vantage points reduce surprise encounters.
  • Keep distance from water sources at dawn and dusk. Large herbivores and predators often gather there.
  • Travel in groups and communicate quietly with hand signals.
  • Know escape routes before you settle. Study terrain features like cliffs, caves, and rivers. More on terrain navigation: Orienteering.
  • Always secure food away from your shelter to avoid attracting curious creatures.
